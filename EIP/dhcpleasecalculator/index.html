DHCP Lease Per Seconds Estimator

This tool is to provide an estimate on the Leases Per Seconds based on the number of clients, lease time and expected growth values. The calculated LPS is a best guessed value to assist in sizing.
NOTE: It is best to capture the accurate value from the existing production environment to determine the required capacity.
